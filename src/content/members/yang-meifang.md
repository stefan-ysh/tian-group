---EN---

Dr. Meifang Yang received her Ph.D. from Sun Yat-sen University and is currently a Lecturer at the School of Chemistry and Materials, Yangzhou University. Her research focuses on the synthesis of novel semiconductor nanoluminescent materials and their applications in optoelectronic devices.
