杨梅芳，博士毕业于中山大学，现为扬州大学化学与材料学院讲师。主要研究方向为新型半导体纳米发光材料的合成、光物理性质调控与光电器件应用。

她的研究关注半导体纳米发光材料的组成设计、形貌调控、发光性能优化与稳定性提升，致力于探索材料结构与光学性质之间的内在关系。通过材料合成、表征分析和器件构筑相结合，推动新型纳米发光材料在显示、照明、传感及相关光电器件中的应用研究。

在教学与科研工作中，杨梅芳注重培养学生的实验能力、材料分析能力和科研思维，鼓励学生从材料设计、性能测试到器件应用开展系统性研究。

---EN---

Dr. Meifang Yang received her Ph.D. from Sun Yat-sen University and is currently a Lecturer at the School of Chemistry and Materials, Yangzhou University. Her research focuses on the synthesis of novel semiconductor nanoluminescent materials, regulation of photophysical properties, and their applications in optoelectronic devices.

Her work is centered on compositional design, morphology control, emission-property optimization, and stability improvement of semiconductor nanoluminescent materials. By combining materials synthesis, structural and optical characterization, and device fabrication, she explores the relationship between nanoscale structure and luminescent performance, with potential applications in display, lighting, sensing, and related optoelectronic devices.

In teaching and research supervision, Dr. Yang emphasizes experimental skills, materials characterization, and scientific thinking, encouraging students to conduct systematic studies from material design and performance evaluation to device-level applications.
